About Open Terminal

Open Terminal is an advanced trading platform designed for traders who value efficiency and simplicity. Built with Python, Flask, and AngelOne Smart API, it delivers powerful tools and seamless user experiences for smarter investments.

Core Features

Realtime Watchlists

Stay updated with live market data and custom watchlists.

Place Orders from Watchlists

Quickly execute trades directly from your watchlists.

Order Book & Holdings

View and manage your positions, orders, and portfolio in one place.

Trailing Stoploss Orders

Automate risk management with trailing stoploss features.

Voice-Based Trading

Execute trades with voice commands for a hands-free experience.

Keyboard-Based Orders

Trade faster with keyboard shortcuts designed for power users.
